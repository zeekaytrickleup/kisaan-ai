## 💡 Expert Recording Tips for a Win
1.  **Use Chrome Screen Recorder**: Use an extension like *Loom* or *OBS Studio* to record your screen in high quality (1080p).
2.  **Speak Confidently**: Keep your voice enthusiastic and energetic. Practice the Roman Urdu prompt a couple of times so typing it feels natural!
3.  **Emphasize the console**: Whenever the MONOSPACE GLOWING terminal outputs something, point it out! Hackathon judges LOVE visible agent traces (`[PLAN]`, `[TOOL]`).
4.  **Test-Click WhatsApp**: When you click the green button, have WhatsApp open in a side tab beforehand to show how beautiful the pre-filled Urdu text looks!
